Our Story

Why NISC Was Founded

In 2022, a group of passionate IT students in Nepalgunj noticed a striking gap — while Kathmandu was buzzing with tech events, bootcamps, and hackathons, the entire far-western region of Nepal had virtually no organized platform for IT students to connect, learn, or grow together.

Students in Nepalgunj, Surkhet, Dang, and surrounding cities were just as talented and motivated — but lacked access to mentorship, collaborative projects, and industry exposure. Many traveled to Kathmandu just to attend a single workshop, while others gave up on tech careers entirely due to isolation.

"We didn't want another student in Banke, Bardiya, or Kailali to feel like technology was only for people in the capital." — NISC Founding Team, 2022

NISC was established to change that. Our purpose is simple but powerful: bring world-class IT learning culture to Nepalgunj and the entire far-western Nepal region — so that geography is never a barrier to a great tech career.

Today, NISC serves students across Nepalgunj, Surkhet, Dang (Tulsipur), Bardiya, Banke, Kailali (Dhangadhi), and Kanchanpur — with plans to expand to every district that wants to be part of this tech movement.

🎯 Our Mission

Build Nepal's Far-West IT Ecosystem

To foster a thriving, inclusive, and collaborative IT culture across Nepalgunj and all far-western cities — through education, mentorship, networking, and real-world projects.
