Our Story

Why NISC Was Founded

Nepalgunj IT Student Community (NISC) was started by Sushant Budha and Bharat Rawal in 2023 with a simple but powerful idea — to create a platform where students and young people interested in IT could connect, learn, and grow together.

In places like Nepalgunj and nearby regions, many students are interested in technology but often lack proper guidance, exposure, and opportunities. Most learning happens individually, without a strong community to support or guide the journey. Realizing this gap, we decided to take a step forward and build something that could bring people together.

"We didn't want another student in Banke, Bardiya, or Kailali to feel like technology was only for people in the capital." — NISC Founding Team, 2022

NISC was not created as a big organization from the beginning. It started as a small initiative with a vision to build a supportive IT environment in Nepalgunj. The goal was simple — organize sessions, invite mentors, share knowledge, and create a space where learning feels practical and accessible.

From the early days, we focused on real conversations rather than just theory. Whether it’s through meetups, online sessions, or collaborations, the aim has always been to provide value and encourage people to take action in their own journey.

One important realization we had along the way is that a community cannot grow with just two people. It needs active participation, ideas, and support from everyone involved. NISC is not just about the founders — it is about every individual who joins, contributes, and believes in building something meaningful together.

"Today, NISC is growing step by step, with the vision to create a strong IT community not only in Nepalgunj but also in nearby regions. The journey has just started, and the focus remains the same — learning, sharing, and growing together.>"
🎯 Our Mission

Build Nepal's Far-West IT Ecosystem

Our mission at Nepalgunj IT Student Community (NISC) is to build a strong, supportive, and growth-focused IT community where students and young individuals can learn, connect, and develop real-world skills.

We aim to create opportunities for learning beyond classrooms by organizing sessions, meetups, and collaborations that provide practical knowledge and industry exposure. Through this, we want to help individuals gain clarity in their career paths and build confidence in their skills.

NISC is focused on making technology more accessible by creating a platform where anyone — regardless of their background — can explore, ask questions, and grow together with like-minded people.

We also believe in the power of community. Our mission is not only to teach but to encourage participation, collaboration, and support among members, so that everyone can contribute and benefit from the shared knowledge.

In the long run, we aim to contribute to the growth of the IT ecosystem in Nepalgunj and nearby regions by building a culture of learning, innovation, and continuous improvement.
