About NISC

NISC (Nepalgunj IT Student Community) is a free, non-commercial, volunteer-run student tech community based in Nepalgunj, Banke, Nepal. Founded in 2022, NISC aims to empower IT students and tech enthusiasts across far-western Nepal.

NISC is not a registered company, educational institution, or employment agency. We do not charge membership fees. All events, workshops, and mentorship programs are provided free of charge (unless explicitly stated otherwise for specific materials).

NISC does not guarantee employment, internships, or career outcomes. Any opportunities shared are done in good faith as community resources.

3
Membership

NISC membership is open to anyone interested in technology, regardless of age, gender, background, or skill level. Membership is completely free.

By becoming a member, you agree to:

  • Provide accurate information during signup — do not register with fake names or details
  • Keep your contact information up to date
  • Represent yourself honestly in all community interactions
  • Not create duplicate or multiple accounts
  • Not share your WhatsApp group invite links or Discord links with people outside NISC without permission
Membership is free forever. NISC will never charge you for basic membership. If you are ever asked to pay for NISC membership by someone claiming to represent NISC, please report it to niscnpj@gmail.com immediately.

Community Rules

NISC is built on respect, collaboration, and learning. All members must follow these rules across all NISC platforms (WhatsApp, Discord, Facebook, events, and this website).

✅ Allowed & Encouraged
🤝 Respectful, constructive discussions
💡 Sharing knowledge, resources, opportunities
🙋 Asking questions at any skill level
🎓 Sharing your projects and progress
📢 Promoting tech events relevant to members
🤲 Volunteering to help other members
❌ Strictly Prohibited
🚫 Harassment, bullying, or personal attacks
🚫 Hate speech, discrimination of any kind
🚫 Sharing adult, violent, or inappropriate content
🚫 Spam, unsolicited promotions, or scams
🚫 Sharing pirated software, illegal content
🚫 Impersonating NISC or other members

Additional rules for our online platforms:

  • WhatsApp groups: Keep messages relevant. Avoid flooding the group. Do not add people to the group without NISC admin permission.
  • Discord server: Use the correct channels. Follow channel-specific rules. Respect all members regardless of skill level.
  • Social media: Do not tag NISC in inappropriate content. Do not post misinformation about NISC.
Violations of community rules may result in a warning, temporary suspension, or permanent removal from NISC, at the discretion of the NISC core team. Serious violations (harassment, threats, illegal activity) may be reported to relevant authorities.

Events & Workshops

By registering for a NISC event, you agree to the following:

  • Accurate registration: Register only for yourself with your real name and contact details
  • Attendance commitment: If you register and cannot attend, please notify us at least 24 hours in advance so we can offer your seat to someone else
  • Conduct at events: All community rules apply at physical and online events. Disruptive behavior will result in removal from the event
  • Photography & recording: NISC may photograph or record events for community documentation. By attending, you consent to potentially appearing in such content. Notify us before the event if you have concerns.
  • Materials: Workshop materials shared by speakers are for personal learning only — do not redistribute without the speaker's permission
Cancellations: NISC reserves the right to cancel, reschedule, or change the format of any event due to unforeseen circumstances. We will notify registered attendees as soon as possible via email or WhatsApp.

For paid events (if any, clearly marked with the fee): Fees cover material costs only. Refund policies will be stated clearly at the time of registration for any paid event.

Termination of Membership

You may leave NISC at any time by emailing niscnpj@gmail.com requesting removal. We will delete your data within 7 business days.

NISC may remove members who violate these terms. Grounds for removal include:

  • Repeated or serious violations of community rules
  • Harassment or threats directed at any member, speaker, or volunteer
  • Fraudulent or misleading registration information
  • Using NISC platforms for commercial gain without permission
  • Any illegal activity

In most cases, we will issue a warning before removing a member. For serious violations (harassment, threats, illegal activity), removal may be immediate without prior notice.

Removal decisions are made by the NISC core team. If you believe you were removed unfairly, you may appeal by emailing niscnpj@gmail.com.
