Plain English Summary: NISC is a free student tech community. We collect your name, contact details, and interests only to run events and send you relevant updates. We never sell your data. You can ask us to delete your information at any time by emailing niscnpj@gmail.com.
1
Who We Are

NISC (Nepalgunj IT Student Community) is a free, volunteer-run, non-commercial student tech community based in Nepalgunj, Banke, Nepal. We operate across far-western Nepal, including Surkhet, Dhangadhi, Dang, Bardiya, and surrounding districts.

We are not a registered company or corporation. NISC is a community initiative founded in 2022 to support IT students and tech enthusiasts in far-western Nepal through free workshops, mentorship, events, and networking.

Contact: For all privacy-related questions, email us at niscnpj@gmail.com.

2
Data We Collect

We collect information only when you voluntarily provide it — for example, when you join NISC or register for an event. We do not collect data silently or without your knowledge.

Data Type Examples When Collected
Identity First name, last name Membership signup, event registration
Contact Email address, phone number Membership signup, event registration
Location City / district Membership signup, event registration
Profile Age range, institution, field of study, skill level Membership signup
Interests Tech interests, goals, learning preferences Membership signup
Event Data Event preferences, attendance type, expectations Event registration
Usage (basic) Pages visited (no personal tracking) Automatically when visiting our website
What we do NOT collect: We do not collect passwords, financial information, ID numbers, biometric data, or any sensitive personal information. We do not use tracking pixels or behavioral advertising tools.
3
How We Use Your Data

We use your information strictly for community operations. Here is exactly how:

  • To confirm your membership — sending a welcome email after you join NISC
  • To manage event registrations — confirming your spot, sending event details, reminders, and follow-ups
  • To send community updates — newsletters, upcoming event announcements, workshop invites (you can unsubscribe anytime)
  • To add you to WhatsApp groups — only if you consented during signup
  • To match you with mentors — using your skill level and interests to connect you with relevant mentors
  • To improve our programs — understanding what skills our members want helps us plan better workshops
  • To respond to your queries — when you contact us directly
We will never: Use your data for advertising, sell it to companies, share it for marketing purposes, or use it for any purpose not described in this policy without asking you first.
4
Data Sharing

We take data sharing seriously. Your data may be accessed by:

  • NISC core team members — volunteer organizers who manage events and community operations, strictly on a need-to-know basis
  • Google (data storage) — your form submissions are stored in Google Sheets, which is governed by Google's Privacy Policy
  • Event speakers/mentors — only your name and contact details, and only if you registered for their specific session, with your prior consent

We do not share your data with:

  • Advertisers or marketing companies
  • Recruiters or employers (without your explicit request)
  • Any third party for commercial purposes
  • Government agencies (unless legally required)
5
Data Storage & Security

Your submitted data is stored in Google Sheets secured to the NISC Gmail account (niscnpj@gmail.com). Access is restricted to NISC core team members only.

We take the following precautions to protect your data:

  • Google account is secured with two-factor authentication (2FA)
  • Spreadsheet access is restricted — not publicly accessible
  • Only authorized NISC team members can view member data
  • We regularly review who has access and remove inactive team members
Retention: We keep your data for as long as you are an active NISC member. If you request deletion, we will remove your information from our records within 7 business days.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your data, no internet transmission is 100% secure. If you believe your data has been compromised, please contact us immediately at niscnpj@gmail.com.

6
Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access — You can request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Right to Correction — You can ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Right to Deletion — You can request complete deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Opt-Out — You can unsubscribe from emails or WhatsApp updates at any time
  • Right to Object — You can object to how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability — You can request your data in a readable format (CSV/PDF)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at niscnpj@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Request – [Your Name]". We will respond within 7 business days.

7
Cookies

Our website is a static HTML site and currently does not use cookies for tracking, analytics, or advertising.

If we ever add analytics tools (such as Google Analytics), we will update this policy and add a cookie consent notice before doing so. You will always be notified and given a choice.

8
Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external platforms such as Facebook, Discord, WhatsApp, GitHub, LinkedIn, and YouTube. When you click these links, you leave NISC's website and are governed by those platforms' own privacy policies.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any third-party websites. We encourage you to review their policies before sharing personal information on those platforms.

9
Children's Privacy

NISC welcomes students of all ages, including those under 16 (our "Under 16" age group). For members under 16, we strongly recommend parental awareness of their participation in our community.

We do not knowingly collect sensitive data from children beyond what is needed for community participation (name, email, phone for event coordination). If a parent or guardian believes their child's data has been collected inappropriately, please contact us at niscnpj@gmail.com and we will promptly delete it.

10
Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will change
  • Significant changes will be announced via our WhatsApp community group and email newsletter
  • Continued use of NISC services after changes implies acceptance of the updated policy

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Previous versions of this policy are available upon request.
